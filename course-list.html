Pumps and Hydraulics

This course is designed to train operators in the fundamental aspects of pumps and hydraulics as they relate to the operation of water, wastewater treatment plants, collection and distribution systems. The knowledge gained in this course should aid the student in preparing for the appropriate state operator certification examination.
