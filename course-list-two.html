Drinking Water Regulations

The EPA regulates more than 100 biological and chemical substances, and more are being added each year. The EPA is continuously revising standards. In addition, there are treatment technique requirements that apply in lieu of MCLs. This courae will review the Safe Drinking Water Act and the most current regulations.
