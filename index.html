About WWCourses

Our Onlinecourses are designed to educate and familiarize students with the fundamentals of the operation of water treatment plants, wasterwater treatment plants, water distribution systems, and wastewater collection systems. The knowledge gained in these courses will aid the student in preparing for the operator certification examination.

Suggested Courses

Intro to Laboratory

This course provides an overview of the equipment, techniques and analytical methods used by operators of water and wastewater treatment plants. This course will provide the student with an understanding of the equipment, techniques and analytical methods used by operators of water and wastewater treatment plants.

  • $35

Activated Sludge

This course is designed to train operators in the aspects of the Activated Sludge Process used in wastewater treatment plants. This course will provide the student with an understanding of the aspects of the Activated Sludge Process. The knowledge gained in this course will help prepare for the Activated Sludge wastewater operator exam.

  • $75

WASTEWATER COLLECTION SYSTEM

This course is designed to train operators in the practical aspects of wastewater collection system operation and maintenance (O&M), with an emphasis on safe practices. The knowledge gained in this course should aid the student in preparing for the appropriate state wastewater collection certification examination.


  • $75
