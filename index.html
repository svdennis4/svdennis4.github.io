About Us

Established in 2026, Lyon's Deli hopes to become a neighborhood favorite for comfort food, ice cream, and quick trip needs. It is the kind of place where regulars feel at home and first-time guests are treated like old friends.

Built around hospitality, warmth, and familiar flavors, Lyon's Deli brings the local shoppe vibes back to their hometown. The atmosphere is casual, inviting, and full of personality.

Whether you are stopping in for a meal, getting a scoop of ice cream, or just grabbing a quick snack, you will feel like you are right at home.
